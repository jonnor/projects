
## Concept

MiniPC with GPU for VESA mounting on display.

- Open case
- VESA mount in center. 
- Aiming for 5 cm depth, fitting behind 27 inch screen
- Using flat sheet, CNC cut as the base.
- 3d printed brackets where needed 

Key components

- Meanwell PSU, 12v. Used for GPU and PC
- Lenovo Tiny PC, with Oculink
- Nvidia GPU two-slot

Parts list

- NVidia RTX 2060. Or  Want under 250 mm long
- Lenovo M920q or M910q, or M720q
- Lenovo PCIE adapter
- PCIE to Oculink adapter
- Oculink eGPU mount
- Meanwell PSU
- IEC C14 cable adapter
- Lenovo DC cable pigtail
