

## Spear: A Meshtastic throwing node for trees 

Intended for rapid deployment outdoors.
For semi-permanent installations. Over a few days.

Rechargable battery, but not designed to charge continiously (solar etc).

Designed for XIAO ESP32-S3 SX1262 kit.

Using TPU as the outer layer.
